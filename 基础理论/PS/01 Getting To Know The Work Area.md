## 二、组件
1. guides
	- help remind you to keep important video content away from frame edges, because some TVs (typically older ones) slightly crop the image.
	- help align items in a design
	- ![[Pasted image 20260312152616.png]]

2. transform box 变换框 
	- also called a bounding box
	- with handles that let you move, resize, or rotate the image as you place it.
	- Some features in Photoshop happen in a special mode, such as the transform box you just used. Committing your changes exits that mode and returns you to the general Photoshop feature set.
	- ![[Pasted image 20260314201702.png]]
