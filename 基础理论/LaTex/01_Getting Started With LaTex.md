## 1. 名字和读音
- What it shouldn’t sound like is “teks.” For LaTeX, it’s similar; we pronounce it like “Lahtech” or “Laytech.”
- LaTex在Tex的基础上写的，1980s，Tex源于希腊语，代表technology、art、craft
## 2. 优势
### separation of form and content
the author shouldn't be distracted by formatting while writting.
### Portability
它的可移植性体现在三个层面：
- your source file
- its implementation
	- Tex distributions，包含了compiler engines、tools、styles，Tex Live
	- LaTex-specific editors，TeXworks
- the final output
	- PDF，DVI，PostScript，HTML
### 稳定
向后兼容，LaTeX promises that the document will always be readable and consistently produce the same output.
