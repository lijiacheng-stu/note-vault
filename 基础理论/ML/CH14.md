## 没看懂
 a [2018 paper](https://homl.info/elmo)⁠[13](https://learning.oreilly.com/library/view/hands-on-machine-learning/9798341607972/ch14.html#id3298) by Matthew Peters introduced _Embeddings from Language Models_ (ELMo): these are contextualized word embeddings learned from the internal states of a deep bidirectional RNN language model. In other words, instead of just using pretrained word embeddings in your model, you can reuse several layers of a pretrained language model.
